ABOUT NANO

Nano features a spam detection system, word and invite filtering along with other moderation tools.
Apart from that it has many fun and useful commands to brighten up your day (Steam, Team Fortress 2, Minecraft, osu!)
It can even search on Urban Dictionary or Wikipedia for you!

Nano is actively updated by DefaltSimon, the creator.
