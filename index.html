ABOUT NANO

Nano features a wide variety of features ranging from useful commands to moderation tools to help server admins.
Apart from that it has many fun commands to brighten up your day (Steam, Team Fortress 2, Minecraft, osu!)
It can even search on Urban Dictionary or Wikipedia for you, show you a random xkcd comic, or tell you a chuck norris joke!

Nano is actively updated by DefaltSimon, the creator.
